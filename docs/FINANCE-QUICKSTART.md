# NuRock Due Diligence — Finance Team Quick Start

A 5‑minute guide to tracking closing due diligence for every LIHTC deal.

**App:** https://nurockduediligence.vercel.app
**Sign in:** Enter your NuRock email → you'll get a 6‑digit code by email → enter it. (Same login as the underwriting and development apps.) If you see a "no access" screen, ask an admin to add you under **Settings → Users & Access**.

---

## The big picture
**Portfolio → Deal → Checklist.**
1. **Deals** (home) lists every deal with its **DD Readiness %**.
2. Click a deal → its **Due Diligence checklist** opens — 59 standard NuRock items grouped by category (Org Docs, Title/Survey, Environmental, LIHTC, Insurance, Construction, Financing, etc.).
3. Work each item: assign it, set a due date, upload the document(s), and sign it off.

> You can also jump straight here from the portfolio dashboard in the underwriting model: each deal row has a **Diligence ›** button.

---

## Working a checklist item
Click any item row to open it. In the panel you can:
- **Status** — `Not started → In progress → Submitted → Approved`. (Use **N/A** or **Waived** when an item doesn't apply — you'll be asked for a short reason, and it's removed from the readiness math so it doesn't count against you.)
- **Assignee** — pick a team member. They get a notification (and an email if email is enabled).
- **Due date** — overdue items turn red on the list.
- **Upload documents** — drag in a PDF/Office/image file. It's auto‑renamed to `Deal – Item# Title – date` so everything stays tidy. One file can be attached to multiple items.
- **Sign‑off chain** — Preparer → Reviewer → **Approver**. When the Approver approves, the item flips to **Approved** automatically.

**Bulk actions:** select multiple rows (checkboxes) to assign, set status, or export them all at once. Use the filters (status / category / owner / overdue) to focus.

---

## Readiness %
The headline number = **approved required items ÷ applicable required items**. Waived/N‑A items drop out of the denominator. You'll see it three places: the **portfolio** list, the **checklist header** (with a coverage ring + outstanding/overdue counts), and per **investor/lender packet**.

---

## Investor & Lender packets
Different financiers have their own checklists. Instead of re‑tracking everything:
1. **Import their checklist once** (Settings → **Diligence Templates** → **Import checklist**) from an Excel/CSV file — map the columns (which one is the item title, etc.).
2. **Map it to our standard list** (open the template → for each of their items, click the suggested ✨ NuRock item, or pick one). This is the "crosswalk."
3. On a deal, click **+ Add packet** to attach that financier. Now its **coverage %** updates automatically as you approve the underlying standard items — a document attached once counts toward every packet that needs it.

Items the financier wants that we don't capture show an amber **"unmapped"** flag so nothing slips through.

---

## Exporting a packet
Checklist header → **Export packet**:
- **PDF** — a branded summary (readiness, packet coverage, every item with status/owner/due).
- **ZIP** (leave "Bundle linked documents" checked) — the PDF **plus every uploaded file**, renamed to its checklist label. This is what you send an investor or lender.

There's also **Export CSV** for a quick spreadsheet of the checklist.

---

## Reminders
Once a week, anyone with **open or overdue** items assigned to them gets a reminder (in‑app, and email if enabled). You can also nudge an owner anytime from the **"Outstanding by owner"** chips in the checklist header.

---

## Tips
- **N/A vs. leaving blank:** mark genuinely inapplicable items **N/A** (with a reason) so your readiness % reflects reality.
- **Attach once, link many:** if one document satisfies several items, attach it to each — readiness and packet coverage both pick it up.
- **LIHTC deadlines:** the checklist header shows countdowns (Placed‑in‑Service, 8609, conversion, etc.) pulled from the deal's underwriting dates — keep an eye on the amber/red chips.
- **Questions / access:** admins manage access under **Settings → Users & Access**.
